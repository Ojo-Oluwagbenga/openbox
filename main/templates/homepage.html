How does it work?

  • Select the closest box

    When you chose a box, a use context is opened for you with the box. You can set your password, send your documents for printing and track your packages

  • Get your package

    Get to the box to pick your document or package, you can request that your package be moved to your location

  • Checkout

    After your package is extracted, you can free up the box to pause the billing for future use
